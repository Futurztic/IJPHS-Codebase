About Us

The International Journal of Public Health Sciences is a journal for publishing high calibre thesis. Health and medicine will be the primary research topics, which will later be beneficial to compare, study, and plan for the development of particular fields of medicines and health.

Each thesis will be examined for journalism standards, to see if it satisfies the broad requirements and to ensure that the material is appropriate in cases where the research produces meaningful findings. To maintain the calibre and standard of the thesis we publish on our platform, the review procedure will be carried out by qualified medical editors from various institutions.
